Welcome to Learning Lab!

Our aim is to provide a better learning environment so that learners at any age can enjoy learning and prosper in their fields of interest.

We are esperically interested in the social aspect of learning where leaners learn from and with others. Being a social animal, humans learn from others at their early stage of life and learn to learn with others as soon as they get along with peers and become natural social learners. The situation changes abruptly as they get into a public, (claim to be) impartial, and standardized system. The joy of learning togither soon gets lost. Our interest is to find ways to rekindle the joy of learning with others.

Our methods encompass both quantitative and qualitative. We use machine learning approach to discern hidden patterns of human behaviors and cognitive and psychological approach to analyze discourses among learners.
